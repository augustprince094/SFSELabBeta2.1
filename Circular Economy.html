Industrial Circular Bioeconomy

Under this research theme, we perform an overall-sustainability assessment of product and process systems, identify and characterize co-products and by-products, and simulate and identify pathways within the value chain for upcycling and circularity considering social, environmental, and economic implications


Our research activities under this theme include

  • Circularity of cashew fruits
  • Alternative utilization pathways for starch co/by-products from protein extaction
  • Building virtual models (digital twins) of new products and process designs geared towards upcycling and circularity
