What we do



The core of our research is to conduct, develop tools and support sustainability assessment at the CONSUMER and INDUSTRIAL level.

At the CONSUMER level, we focus on bridging the gap between environmental sustainability and nutrition.

At the INDUSTRIAL level, we focus on employing life cycle sustainability assessment tools to evaluate the performance of agri-food and bio-product systems.

In achieving this, we leverage digital innovations, machine learning, computational modelling, and human health impact modeling to drive a sustainable and resilient food systems.
