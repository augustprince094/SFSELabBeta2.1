Environmental Nutriomics Assessment

Under this research theme, we employ environmental life cycle assessment, nutrient profiling and health performance modeling to determine the simultenous health and environmental implication consumer food choices.


Some of our research activities under this theme include

  • Development of decision support systems to stimulate sustainable food choices
  • Multi-objective modeling of the environmental and health implications food choices
  • Consequencies of transitioning to sustainable healthy diets
